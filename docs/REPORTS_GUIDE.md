# Reports & Dashboards — Plain-English Guide

This guide explains **every dashboard and every report (widget)** in the platform:
what it shows, and **why it matters** for running a day nursery in London. It is
written for nursery owners, managers and staff — no technical knowledge needed.

> The whole platform answers the 5 questions a London nursery operator needs
> instantly:
> 1. **Are we full or empty?** (Occupancy)
> 2. **Are we getting paid?** (Finance)
> 3. **Do we have enough staff?** (Staff)
> 4. **Are we inspection-ready?** (Compliance)
> 5. **Are children safe, present and progressing?** (Attendance, EYFS)

## How to read the dashboards

- **KPI card** — a single headline number (e.g. *Occupancy 87%*). A small line under
  it is a **sparkline** (recent trend); a coloured **▲/▼** is the change vs the
  previous period; an **Alert** badge flags something needing attention.
- **Gauge** — a 0–100% dial. Green = healthy, amber = watch, red = act.
- **Trend line** — how a number moves over time; a dashed line is a **forecast**.
- **Bar / stacked bar** — comparisons (e.g. by room, by site).
- **Pie / donut** — a breakdown of a whole (e.g. funded vs private places).
- **Heatmap** — a grid where colour = intensity (e.g. attendance by day & room).
- **Funnel** — a drop-off process (e.g. enquiry → waitlist → enrolled).

## Filters (top of each dashboard)

- **Site** — view all sites combined, or focus on one nursery. (Single-site staff
  are automatically limited to their own site.)
- **Period** — 7 days / 30 days / 90 days / 12 months. It only appears on dashboards
  with *activity-over-time* reports (Occupancy, Finance, Staff, Attendance, EYFS,
  Parent Comms, Nutrition, Analytics) and changes those numbers (e.g. new admissions,
  absences, payments collected).
- **Live snapshot** — "as of today" dashboards (Executive, Multi-Site, Operations,
  Mobile) show a *Live snapshot · as of today* badge instead of a period control,
  because a lookback window doesn't apply to current-state numbers like *children on
  roll* or *outstanding arrears* (just as a bank balance isn't a date range).
- **Click-to-filter** — clicking a site in a chart filters the whole dashboard to it.

# 1. Executive Overview  *(Owner / Director)*

**Purpose:** the one screen a busy owner checks each morning.

| Report | What it shows | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|
| Enrolled vs Capacity | Children on roll against licensed places | Your single biggest revenue driver — empty places are lost income |
| Occupancy Rate | % of places filled | The headline health metric; below ~80% margins get tight |
| Revenue (MTD) | Money invoiced this month (+ vs last month) | Are we on track financially this month? |
| Revenue per Child | Average monthly fee income per child | Detects fee/funding erosion; benchmark across sites |
| Outstanding Arrears | Unpaid + overdue balances | Cash you’ve earned but not collected — chase it |
| Profit Estimate (MTD) | Income − payroll − overhead | A live, rough profit signal before the accountant’s month-end |
| Waiting List | Children waiting for a place | Future revenue + which rooms to expand |
| Staff Coverage | Safe / At-risk ratio status | Legal & safety headline at a glance |
| Revenue Trend & Forecast | 12-month revenue + 3-month projection | Spot momentum or decline early |
| Occupancy by Site | Each nursery’s fill rate | Which branch needs attention |
| Alerts Summary | Top open risks (compliance, staffing, finance) | Your action list for today |

# 2. Enrollment & Occupancy  *(Manager / Owner)*

**Purpose:** “Are we full? Where are we losing capacity?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Capacity vs Filled | Filled places vs licensed capacity — the core utilisation number |
| Occupancy Rate | % full overall |
| Funded Hours Mix | Private vs 15h vs 30h government-funded places — affects revenue per child and DfE funding claims |
| New Admissions (30d) | Children starting — is the pipeline working? |
| Withdrawals (30d) | Children leaving — early warning of churn |
| Occupancy by Room Type | Baby / Toddler / Preschool fill vs capacity — babies are costliest, so empty baby places hurt most |
| Age Distribution | Children by age band — plan room moves and staffing |
| Waitlist Conversion | Enquiry → waitlisted → enrolled funnel — where you lose prospective families |
| Occupancy Forecast (6m) | Projected occupancy — plan staffing & marketing ahead |

# 3. Financial & Billing  *(Accounts / Owner)*

**Purpose:** “Are we making money and getting paid?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Billed This Month | Total invoiced |
| Collected This Month | Payments actually received |
| Outstanding Debt | Total owed to you right now |
| Payment Success Rate | % of payment attempts that succeed — Direct Debit/card failures leak cash |
| Paid vs Unpaid | Invoice status split |
| Aged Receivables | Debt by how overdue it is (0–30 / 31–60 / 61–90 / 90+ days) — the older, the harder to recover |
| Revenue Breakdown | Net fees vs government funding vs discounts per month |
| Funded Hours Mix | Share of income from 15h/30h funding vs private fees |
| Revenue per Child | Average monthly income per child |
| Profit per Room Type | Which rooms contribute most |
| Late Payment Alerts | Specific overdue invoices to chase |

# 4. Staff Management  *(Manager / Owner)*

**Purpose:** “Do we have enough qualified staff to run safely?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Staff On Duty Today | Headcount present now |
| Ratio Compliance | % of rooms meeting the EYFS adult:child ratio — a legal must (1:3 babies, 1:4 toddlers, 1:8 preschool) |
| Absence Rate (30d) | Shifts lost to absence — drives agency cost and ratio risk |
| Overtime Hours (30d) | Overtime worked — a cost and burnout signal |
| Ratio by Room | Actual vs required children-per-staff per room |
| Qualification Mix | Spread of EYFS Level 2/3/6 — Ofsted expects a qualified team |
| Staff Utilisation Trend | Hours worked vs scheduled |
| Payroll Cost (30d) | Estimated staff cost — your largest expense |
| Agency Usage | Share of hours covered by agency — expensive; high use signals recruitment gaps |

# 5. Compliance & Regulatory  *(Manager / Owner)* — Ofsted-focused

**Purpose:** “Are we inspection-ready today?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Audit Readiness Score | Composite of DBS validity, incident closure & open high-risk items |
| Open Incidents | Accidents/incidents not yet closed (high-severity flagged) |
| DBS Checks | Valid / expiring / expired staff DBS — expired = a child cannot be left in their sole care |
| Incidents by Type | Accident / safeguarding / medication / other |
| Incident Trend | Reported vs closed over time — are you keeping up? |
| Ofsted Checklist | Status across safeguarding, medication, risk assessments, training, policies |

# 6. Child Development (EYFS)  *(Teacher / Manager / Parent)*

**Purpose:** “Are children progressing across the EYFS framework?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Children On Track | % meeting expected development |
| Observations (period) | Number of learning observations logged — engagement of practitioners |
| At-Risk Children | Children below expected level — need targeted support |
| Progress by Area | The EYFS areas (communication, physical, PSE, literacy, numeracy) |
| Progress by Age Group | Emerging / expected / exceeding mix per age band |
| Development Heatmap | Area × age-group on-track % — spot weak spots in the curriculum |

# 7. Attendance & Check-in  *(Teacher / Manager)*

**Purpose:** “Who is actually present?”

| Report | What / Why |
|---|---|
| Present Today | Live headcount |
| Attendance Rate | Present vs expected over the period |
| Absent Today | Illness / holiday / unexplained |
| Late Pickups | Late collections — a safeguarding & staffing-cost issue |
| Attendance Trend | Daily attendance rate (shows term-time vs holiday dips) |
| Absence Reasons | Breakdown of why children are away |
| Utilisation by Session | Average **AM vs PM** headcount — afternoon gaps = sellable spare capacity |
| Attendance Heatmap | Day-of-week × room — find consistently quiet sessions |

# 8. Parent Communication  *(Manager / Teacher)*

Messages volume, average staff response time, read rate, daily reports sent, and an
inbound-vs-outbound trend. **Why:** engaged, well-informed parents renew and refer;
slow responses are the #1 complaint driver.

# 9. Meal, Health & Nutrition  *(Teacher / Manager / Parent)*

Average meal intake, allergy alerts, meals logged, and intake by meal.
**Why:** wellbeing evidence for parents and Ofsted; allergy visibility is safety-critical.

# 10. Multi-Site Management  *(Owner / Director)*

Site performance **ranking** (composite score), occupancy by site, revenue by site.
**Why:** see best/worst performing nurseries at a glance and share what works.

# 11. Analytics & BI  *(Owner / Accounts)*

Occupancy trend, revenue growth, staff-cost-to-revenue ratio, and the enquiry→
enrollment funnel. **Why:** spot trends and seasonality to act on, not just today’s number.

# 12. Alerts & Risk  *(Manager / Owner)*

A prioritised feed of what needs attention **now** — ratio breaches, expired DBS,
overdue payments, unexplained absences — with severity counts. **Why:** one place
for “what could bite us today”.

# 13. Operations Control Panel  *(Manager / Teacher)*

Today’s present count, staff on duty, and the live rota. **Why:** the daily “who’s
in, who’s where” operational view.

# 14. Mobile Parent / Staff  *(Parent)*

A parent sees **their own child only**: today’s status and recent updates (meals,
naps, activities). **Why:** the parent-app summary that drives daily engagement.

# 15. Advanced AI / Smart  *(Owner / Manager)*

Predicted occupancy gaps, projected staffing shortfalls, and parent-churn risk
(families with falling attendance). **Why:** act *before* a place empties or a family
leaves. *(These are statistical projections from your own data, not a black box.)*

## Who sees what (default roles)

| Role | Default dashboards |
|---|---|
| **Administrator** | Everything + user & dashboard configuration |
| **Management / Owner** | All analytics dashboards (1–13, 15) |
| **Accounts** | Finance, Occupancy, Executive, Analytics, Alerts |
| **Teacher / Practitioner** | Attendance, EYFS, Nutrition, Parent Comms, Operations (their site) |
| **Parent** | Mobile + their own child’s Attendance, EYFS, Nutrition, Comms |

Admins can change any of this in **Roles & Dashboards** (`/admin`): turn whole
dashboards on/off per role (Module access) or individual cards (Dashboard widgets).
See [DATABASE.md](DATABASE.md) for how this is stored and
[DATA_INTEGRATION.md](DATA_INTEGRATION.md) for connecting your real data.
